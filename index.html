About Us

Art rooted in

The Shining Light was born from a love of Islamic art and heritage — the belief that every home deserves a piece that carries meaning beyond its beauty.

Each item in our collection is thoughtfully selected or created to bring authenticity, elegance, and cultural depth into your space. We work directly with artisans and trusted makers who share our commitment to craft.

Based in Singapore, we deliver across the region. Every order is handled personally, and we're always just a WhatsApp message away.
